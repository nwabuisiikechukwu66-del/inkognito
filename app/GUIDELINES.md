# Inkognito: The Anonymous Social Experience

Welcome to Inkognito, a premium social platform built for the shadows. This guide explains what the app is, how it works, and how to get the most out of your anonymous presence.

## 🌚 What is Inkognito?
Inkognito is a platform designed for raw, honest, and unfiltered human expression. Unlike traditional social media, Inkognito has no profiles, no personal data tracking, and no permanence by default. You are defined only by your words and your interactions in the moment.

## 🚀 How it Works
1.  **Anonymous Sessions**: When you first visit, we generate a unique, cryptographically secure **Session ID** for you. This ID is stored only on your device. We don't ask for your name, email, or phone number.
2.  **Confessions**: Share your thoughts, secrets, or observations. Your post is tagged with a category and visible to the global feed immediately.
3.  **Real-time Interactions**: React to others using our rich reaction set, or join the conversation with anonymous comments.
4.  **AI Companion**: Your personal AI assistant is always available for deep conversations. Your chat history is synced across your devices if you choose to link them.

## ✨ Premium Features (Inkognito Plus)
For $3/mo, you can upgrade your experience:
*   **Custom Usernames**: Claim a permanent alias that travels with you across the platform.
*   **Direct Messaging**: Start 1-on-1 private conversations with anyone on the feed.
*   **Priority Support**: Your feedback and reports are handled with high priority.

## 🔄 New Features: Bookmarks & Echoes
*   **Bookmarks**: See something you want to keep? Tap the bookmark icon to save it to your private **Saved** list. These are stored securely and only accessible by you.
*   **Echoes**: Want to amplify someone's voice? **Echo** their confession to the void. Echoing reposts the content, giving it a massive boost in the **Trending** feed and signaling its resonance to the community.
*   **Void Polls**: Authors can attach binary polls to their confessions. Cast your vote anonymously and see where the community stands.
*   **Whispers**: When replying, you can choose to send a **Whisper**. These are private replies that only the author of the confession can see.
*   **Fading Messages**: Need your words to be truly ephemeral? Enable **Fading** on your replies to have them automatically self-destruct after 24 hours.
*   **Shadow Moods**: Set a **Vibe** (like ⚖️ Guilty or 🍃 Relieved) when you post to give the community context on how you're feeling.
*   **Void Streaks**: Stay active! Your **Streak** shows how many consecutive days you've shared or interacted with the void. Check your profile to see your progress.

## 🛡️ Privacy & Safety
*   **Device Sync**: Use our secure QR-based linking system to use the same anonymous identity on your phone, tablet, and laptop.
*   **Moderation**: We use automated tools to block illegal content, but we rely on the community to report harassment or spam.
*   **Purge Identity**: At any time, you can visit **Settings** and select **Purge Local Identity**. This deletes your session data from your device instantly.

*   **Be Kind**: Anonymity is a superpower; use it to build up, not tear down.
*   **Explore the Shadows**: Use the **Discover** page to find confessions from different walks of life.
*   **Resonate**: When a post hits home, **Echo** it. It's the highest form of respect in the void.
